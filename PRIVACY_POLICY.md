# Trade Buddy Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Trade Buddy collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the Trade Buddy mobile app and related services.

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use Trade Buddy:

- Account information, such as your name, email address, and password, when you sign up or sign in.
- Listing information, such as listing type, title, category, condition, location, description, price, and any photo you choose to upload.
- Device-stored information, such as your saved listings and sign-in session information stored locally on your device.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- Create and manage your account.
- Authenticate your access to the app.
- Display and manage marketplace listings.
- Let you create, save, and browse listings.
- Support account actions such as logout and account deletion.
- Enable buyers and sellers to contact each other through their device email app.

## 3. Photos and Device Permissions

Trade Buddy may request access to your photo library when you choose to upload an image for a listing. We only access your photos after you initiate that action.

The current Android app configuration also declares microphone permission. If enabled by the released app build, that permission may appear on your device, although the app code reviewed for this policy is focused on account, listings, and image selection features.

## 4. Local Device Storage

Trade Buddy stores some information locally on your device using app storage, including:

- Your signed-in session and authentication token.
- Basic account information returned by the server.
- IDs of listings you save in the app.

This local storage helps keep you signed in and preserves saved items on your device.

## 5. Remote Services and Data Transmission

Trade Buddy connects to services hosted at mytradebuddy.com to handle:

- Account signup and login.
- Listing retrieval and listing creation.
- Logout requests.
- Account deletion requests.

When you submit data through the app, including listing information and uploaded listing photos, that information is sent to Trade Buddy's backend services.

## 6. Sharing and Visibility

Your listing information may be visible to other Trade Buddy users.

When a user selects Contact Seller, the app opens the user's email application using the seller email associated with the listing. This allows buyer-seller communication outside the app.

We do not state that we sell personal data, run advertising, or use analytics tools in this policy because those behaviors were not identified in the app code reviewed for this document.

## 7. Data Retention and Account Deletion

You can request deletion of your account from the Settings area of the app while signed in.

Based on the current app behavior:

- An account deletion request is sent to the Trade Buddy backend.
- Your local signed-in session is removed from your device.
- Listings and related account data may be removed by the backend handling that request.

Saved listing IDs stored locally on your device may remain until cleared by the app or removed when app data is deleted from the device.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Trade Buddy is intended for school-community marketplace use and is not designed to knowingly collect personal information directly from children without appropriate supervision. If you believe a child has provided personal information improperly, please contact Trade Buddy through the website so the issue can be reviewed.

## 9. Security

We take reasonable steps to support the security of account and listing information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The version published in this repository is the current version provided for the app at the time of publication.

## 11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact Trade Buddy through:

- Website: https://mytradebuddy.com
