Terms of Use

Last updated: April 17, 2026

These terms apply before you access Trade Buddy user-generated content. By continuing to browse or use Trade Buddy, you agree to these Terms of Use and the zero-tolerance policy for objectionable content and abusive users.

1. Eligibility and Access

Trade Buddy is a school-community marketplace intended for parents and guardians. Guests may browse public listings. Creating listings and account management require a registered account.

2. No Tolerance for Objectionable Content

You may not post objectionable content, harassment, hate speech, threats, sexual content, scams, or abusive material. Trade Buddy has zero tolerance for abusive users and objectionable listings.

3. Reporting and Blocking

Users can report objectionable listings and block abusive sellers in the app. Blocking removes that seller from the user feed immediately.

Trade Buddy reviews reports within 24 hours and removes confirmed objectionable content or abusive accounts.

4. Listing Rules

  • Listings must describe genuine school-related items or requests.
  • Listings must use accurate titles, descriptions, and pricing details.
  • Listings may not include profanity, discriminatory language, or unsafe content.

5. Enforcement

Trade Buddy may remove content, suspend accounts, or permanently ban users who violate these terms or create risk for other users.

6. Contact

For questions about these terms or moderation concerns, contact Trade Buddy through mytradebuddy.com or the public support page at support.html.
